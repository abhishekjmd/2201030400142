Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does it work?

Speaky is a language exchange platform that connects people from around the world for language learning and cultural exchange. Users can create a profile, search for language partners, and engage in conversations through text, or voice.

Is it free?

Yes, Speaky is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions required. Simply sign up, create a profile, and start connecting with language partners.

How can I join?

Joining Speaky is easy. Just visit our website or download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Sign up with your email or social media account, complete your profile, and you’re ready to start learning and connecting with others.

Can I learn any language?

Yes, Speaky supports a wide range of languages. Whether you want to learn English, Spanish, French, German, or any other language, you can find language partners who are native speakers of the language you want to learn.

Is it safe?

At Speaky, we prioritize the safety and security of our users. We have implemented measures to ensure a safe and positive language learning experience, such as user verification, word filtering, reporting features, and a dedicated support team.

How can I report abuse?

If you encounter any abusive or inappropriate behavior on Speaky, please report it immediately. You can use the reporting feature within the platform or contact our support team directly. We take reports seriously and will take appropriate action.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes, Speaky has a mobile app available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store and enjoy language learning on the go.

How can I delete my account?

If you no longer wish to use Speaky, you can easily delete your account. Simply go to your account settings, select the option to delete your account, and follow the instructions. Please note that this action is irreversible and will permanently delete all your data.

How can I provide feedback?

We value your feedback and suggestions. You can provide feedback directly through the platform or contact our support team. We appreciate your input as we strive to
improve the Speaky language exchange experience.

Are there language resources?

Yes, Speaky offers a variety of language resources to support your language learning journey. You can access our blog for language lessons, vocabulary lists, and language exchange forums to enhance your language skills.
